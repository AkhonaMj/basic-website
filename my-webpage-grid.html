The Castle of Good Hope

The first stone was laid on 2 January 1666 and it was completed by April 1679 In 1936 the Castle of Good Hope was declared a national monument The Castle of Good Hope was referred to as “Kui keip” (Stone Kraal) by the Khoi and sometimes “Citadel” by Dutch settlers
